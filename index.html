About Us

Providing space in our community for all people to enjoy a just, healthy, and creative life.

We are cyclists, makers, artists and activists

United by the desire to create an equitable and sustainable future for our community

We, at the Spearfish Partnership for Arts, Cycling, and Equity, aim to provide a physical space for the Spearfish Community to create and collaborate. Our programming is accessible to all, regardless of ability to pay. We use a sliding-scale donation model which allows us to pay living wages to our staff and maintain operations for you and our community.

What We Do

We understand there are systemic barriers preventing some people from enjoying a healthy, creative life. We aim to change that.

Makerspace

All people should have the opportunity to experiment and be creative. Our makerspace has salvaged and recycled materials available for all ages and abilities to build and play. The space is open to groups and individuals.

Bike Building and Education

Come to the bike shop to learn about working on and maintaining your bicycle. We have tools for community use, knowledgeable volunteers to answer questions, and parts to build your dream ride.

Equity

We provide a space for people who are often marginalized. We do this through dedicated programs and community education. Join us to learn how you can build bridges and create a more inclusive community.
