Material donations

We are seeking donations of arts and crafts materials.

If you have cycling or art materials you would like to contribute, please reach out to info@makespacesd.org or drop them off during regular open shop hours.

Tools and Supplies:
  • Scissors (Sharp fabric scissors, kids scissors, and scissors for general use)
  • Box cutters and blades
  • Xacto knives and blades
  • Glue (new or nearly new and usable)
  • Paint brushes (various sizes and types)
  • Tape (Duct, masking, painters, packaging tape, washi tape, etc.)
  • Various hand tools (hammers, pliers, screw drivers, snips, etc.)
  • Hot Glue guns and hot glue sticks
  • Extension Cords and power strips
  • Measuring Tapes
  • Mod Podge
  • Knitting needles, crochet hooks, sewing needles, embroidery needles
  • Markers, crayons, colored pencils, oil pastels, watercolor pencils, Sharpies, washable markers, etc.
  • Fabric cutting supplies (rotary cutter, cutting mat, fabric chalk, etc.)
  • Stapler (desktop and staple gun)
  • Storage bins and tubs that can be used for organizing and storing supplies (stackable with lids is a bonus!)
  • Fabric of all types (all must be clean and folded, please)
  • Yarn
  • Beads
  • Embroidery Floss
  • Thread
  • Bailing twine and craft string
  • Buttons
  • Fimo/Sculpey
  • Water based paints for crafting (Acrylic, watercolor, tempera, etc.)
  • Ribbon
  • Popsicle sticks
  • Felt pom-poms of all sizes and colors
  • Pins (straight pins, safety pins, clothes pins, etc.)
  • Feathers (not real ones – the fake colorful ones)
  • Snaps (and snap-maker)
  • Googly eyes
  • Magnets (sheets, circles, etc.)
  • Wire (floral, jewelry weight, electrical, etc.)
  • Stencils
  • Sponges
Recycled and Found Objects:
  • Plastic bottles and jars – CLEAN!!
  • Glass jars – CLEAN!
  • Mason Jars for crafting
  • Costume Jewelry
  • Styrofoam – clean!!
  • Marbles
  • Dowels
  • Metal Cans (clean, with label removed)
  • Toilet paper/Paper Towel tubes, wrapping paper tubes
  • Bottle caps (plastic and metal)
  • Clean plastic containers for crafts
  • Buckets of different sizes
  • Thread spools
  • New shoe laces
  • Pipe cleaners
  • Scrapbook paper, colored paper, and origami paper
  • Rubber bands
  • Corks
  • Calendars, interesting magazines with lots of pictures, colorful greeting card fronts
  • Small crafty objects that could be repurposed
  • Miniature objects
  • Small found sticks, wood, sea glass, rocks, shells, etc.
Other Items:
  • Bicycles (full bikes and quality parts)
  • Working Sewing Machines
  • Soldering iron and solder
  • Iron and Ironing Board (both in good working condition)
  • Paper cutter
  • Button maker
  • Electric Pencil Sharpener
  • Record Player – one that functions and speakers!
  • Folding tables
